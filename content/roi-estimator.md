## Outputs

### Output 1 · Reclaimed Capacity
**≈ 50% – 82% of transactional effort**

Directional band: share of repetitive RCM work that could plausibly shift to agents. Equivalent of ≈ 17.5–28.58 FTE-of-effort across a 35-person team.

### Output 2 · Recovered Revenue Potential
**≈ $7.5M – $15.6M / year** *(at $150M NPR default)*

Combined band: denial recovery + underpayment leakage + eligibility capture. See methodology for the per-component breakdown.

### Output 3 · Growth Headroom

Absorb meaningfully more volume — additional facilities, additional lines of business — without scaling RCM headcount in lock-step with the book.

*Qualitative by design — growth headroom depends on which agents you deploy and in what sequence.*
