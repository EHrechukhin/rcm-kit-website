## The Back-End Loop

Paired agents reinforce each other. The closed loop is where leakage gets caught.

**Step 1 — Payment Posting**  
Posts cash, reconciles to bank, surfaces exceptions. Tells the rest of the loop what actually came in.

**Step 2 — Denials & Appeals**  
Takes posting's denial signal, corrects what's deterministic, drafts strong appeals for the rest — every appeal human-reviewed.

**Step 3 — A/R & Follow-Up**  
Works the rest of the aging book continuously — prioritized, pursued, documented. Closes the loop back to posting.

Each loop node passes signal to the next. The output of one agent is structured input to the others — which is why a coordinated suite beats a kit-bag of point tools.
