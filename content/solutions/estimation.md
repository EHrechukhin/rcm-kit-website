---

## Headline

Produce accurate patient cost estimates up front, improving collections and price transparency.

---

## The Work Today

Estimates are manual, slow, and inconsistent — hurting up-front collection, patient trust, and downstream balance recovery.

---

## What the Agent Does

1. Benefit-driven patient responsibility estimation
2. Estimate generation and delivery support
3. Update estimates on benefit or service changes

---

## How It Works

**Inputs:**
- Service plan
- Verified benefits
- Fee and contract reference data

**Agent Steps:**
1. Compute deductible / co-insurance impact
2. Apply contract rates
3. Produce range and a midpoint
4. Generate patient-facing estimate

**HITL Gate:**  
Estimates above a defined threshold are reviewed before delivery; the agent never sends without a documented basis.

**Outputs:**
- Patient cost estimate
- Internal estimate worksheet
- Audit log of inputs used
