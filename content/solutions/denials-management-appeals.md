## Headline

Turn denials around in days, not weeks — auto-correct the deterministic ones, draft strong appeals for the rest.

---

## The Work Today

Staff manually parse denial and RTP responses, look up payer-specific rules, draft appeal letters from templates, and track status in spreadsheets — while the clock on timely-filing keeps ticking.

---

## What the Agent Does

1. Denial and RTP intake and classification
2. Auto-correction of deterministic denials with documented payer rules
3. Appeal drafting on complex denials for human review
4. Appeal-status tracking through 277CA and portals

---

## How It Works

**Inputs:**
- Denial codes from 835
- RTP responses
- Payer policy library
- Clinical context

**Agent Steps:**
1. Classify denial
2. Apply documented auto-correction where eligible
3. Draft appeal with cited policy and clinical support
4. Track status and re-route

**HITL Gate:**  
Every outgoing appeal is human-reviewed before submission; auto-correction is limited to high-confidence, documented categories.

**Outputs:**
- Corrected and resubmitted claims
- Reviewed and submitted appeals
- Status tracker by claim
