## Headline

Automate patient-responsibility billing and follow-up to recover more of what's owed, sooner.

---

## The Work Today

Patient balances are billed and followed up inconsistently because the team has more pressing work — and quietly written off when they age past attention.

---

## What the Agent Does

1. Patient statement generation on a defined cadence
2. Follow-up workflow with channel selection
3. Income / ability-to-pay verification support
4. Escalation routing on aged balances

---

## How It Works

**Inputs:**
- Patient balance list
- Communication preferences
- Prior contact history

**Agent Steps:**
1. Generate statements
2. Schedule follow-up cadence
3. Capture responses
4. Escalate per rules

**HITL Gate:**  
Escalations and write-down decisions are human-reviewed; the agent suggests, the team approves.

**Outputs:**
- Patient statements
- Follow-up tasks
- Escalation packets

## Where It Connects

- Billing system
- Statement and communication channels
