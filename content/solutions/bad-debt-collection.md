## Headline

Recover more from accounts that would otherwise be written off.

---

## The Work Today

Bad-debt and pre-write-off accounts get inconsistent attention due to capacity limits; whatever recovery happens depends on whose desk it lands on.

---

## What the Agent Does

1. Pre-write-off triage with recovery likelihood
2. Recovery workflow automation
3. Placement and escalation support
4. Documentation and audit

---

## How It Works

**Inputs:**
- Aged accounts
- Prior contact history
- Coverage discovery results

**Agent Steps:**
1. Score recovery likelihood
2. Run workflow
3. Place where appropriate
4. Document outcomes

**HITL Gate:**  
Write-off and placement decisions are human-approved with full agent context.

**Outputs:**
- Triaged accounts
- Recovery actions
- Placement packets
