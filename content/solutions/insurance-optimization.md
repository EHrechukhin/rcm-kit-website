## Headline

Ensure each encounter is billed to the right payer in the right order for the best compliant outcome.

---

## The Work Today

Coordination-of-benefits and payer-order decisions are manual, inconsistent, and time-pressured — leading to mis-sequenced claims, avoidable denials, and longer A/R.

---

## What the Agent Does

1. Payer order / COB optimization at the encounter level
2. Primary, secondary, and tertiary sequencing support
3. Flag suboptimal coverage routing for review

---

## How It Works

**Inputs:**
- Verified coverage list
- Service type and date
- Plan and benefit rules

**Agent Steps:**
1. Apply COB rules to encounter
2. Compute optimal payer order
3. Compare to current routing
4. Flag improvable encounters

**HITL Gate:**  
Re-sequencing recommendations are approved by staff before claims are rebilled; the agent shows the rule, not just the verdict.

**Outputs:**
- Recommended payer order
- Re-routing tasks
- Rule-based explanation
